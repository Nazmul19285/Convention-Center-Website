Takoyaki Convention Center offers Luxurious Banquet Halls, Business Conference Rooms, Business Centre, all of which that are equipped with a modern hygienic Kitchen, car parking facilities and a fantastic and cozy location in the central business district of Gulshan, Dhaka.
